PAINTING MACHINE

Art is too important to not to share

The Tree

The concept of beauty and the beautiful lies in the virtue in which we perceive it, this is an acrylic on canvas, depicting the relationship between the land and the night sky showing the colours of dark, the night sky has all the illuminating stars with the milky way, and the tree standing lonely still is a beauty as it's in a relationship with the universe. Idea is to show that if we accept the universe we become beautiful within and in the eye of all.

It's In Our Hands

It's an acrylic on paper depicting that the progress, prosperity, safety, and the development. It's all in our hands. Everyone is the soldier bearing the flag in their hands. The Flag represents unity and peace that we seek.

The Sky

The painting is acrylic on canvas inspired by the great painter Vincent Van Gogh showing the chaotic life which is still beautiful and enjoyable.

Love

It's an acrylic on canvas showing the love of mother and father towards their offsprings wherein the mothers hand is the feeding hand and the father's hand is blessing. This painting is inspire by Mithlanchal Artform.

Sharing The Knowledge

It is a watercolor on hand made paper. Showing the two scholars sharing the knowledge of the universe with each other. The idea is to grow with each other. The sky here is the universe of knowledge and the two readers are the seekers.

The Reader

Acrylic on canvas. The character depicted here is the reader wherein he is so immersed in the search of knowledge that the candle he was holding first started burning his own hands but as the focus grew the very thing which was the source of pain becomes the shield against it.

Gateway

Acrylic on paper showing the gateway to the ultimate wherein one reaches after walking through various paths of life and at the gate has to cross the two guards of Shiva and Shakti and can only be one with the ultimate once allowed by Lord Ganesh.

Gajraj

Acrylic on canvas. Inspired by Mithlanchal art form. In the paintings are the Sadhus praying to mother nature while Gajraj is there to protect and ensure the completion of the Yagya.
